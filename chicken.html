Chicken Biryani Recipe

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric, salt, lemon juice, and a little oil for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Rinse and soak basmati rice, then boil it with whole spices until about 70% cooked and drain it.
  3. Fry sliced onions in oil or ghee until golden, then add tomatoes (optional), green chilies, and the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is almost done and the gravy thickens.
  4. Layer half the rice in a heavy pot, add the cooked chicken over it, then cover with the remaining rice and sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, ghee, and saffron milk or food color.
  5. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20â€“25 minutes, then let it rest a few minutes before serving.
